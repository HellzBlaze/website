Bhel Puri

How We Make It

Our Bhel Puri is a delightful mix of textures and flavors. We start with a base of puffed rice (murmura) and crispy sev.

To this, we add finely chopped onions, tomatoes, boiled potatoes, and fresh cilantro. The tang comes from our homemade tamarind and date chutney and a spicy green chutney.

Everything is tossed together just before serving to keep the puffed rice and sev crunchy. A final garnish of crushed papdi adds extra crispness. It's a quick, vibrant, and addictive street food snack!
