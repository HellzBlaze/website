Goan Lemon Soda

How We Make It

Our Goan Lemon Soda is a cool escape. We start with freshly squeezed lime juice, not concentrate!

We add a hint of sugar syrup (adjusted to your taste), a few sprigs of fresh mint, and a secret blend of mild spices that give it that unique Goan touch.

This mixture is then combined with chilled sparkling water or club soda, stirred gently, and served over ice. It's a simple, refreshing, and flavorful drink perfect for beating the heat.
