Blue Curaçao Mojito

How We Make It

Our Blue Curaçao Mojito is a cool, colourful, and refreshing drink.

We gently muddle fresh mint leaves and lime wedges in a glass to release their aromas and juices. A touch of simple syrup adds sweetness.

Next, we add crushed ice and pour in a splash of non-alcoholic Blue Curaçao syrup for that vibrant blue hue and a hint of orange flavor.

The glass is then filled with sparkling water or club soda, given a gentle stir, and garnished with more mint and a lime slice. It's a mocktail that's as fun to look at as it is to drink!
